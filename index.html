How to Get Started at Ledger.com/start

  1. Unbox Your Ledger Device
    Once you’ve received your Ledger hardware wallet, unbox the device and ensure all components are intact. Check the packaging for authenticity to confirm it hasn’t been tampered with.

  2. Visit Ledger.com/start
    Navigate to the official website, Ledger.com/start, using a trusted browser. This ensures you access the most up-to-date and secure setup instructions.

  3. Download Ledger Live
    Ledger Live is the official software companion for Ledger devices. Through this application, you can set up your wallet, manage crypto assets, check balances, and access additional features like staking. Download the app directly from Ledger.com/start to avoid third-party risks.

  4. Initialize Your Device
    Follow the guided instructions on Ledger Live to initialize your wallet. This includes creating a PIN code and generating a 24-word recovery phrase. Keep your recovery phrase private and store it securely, as it is the only way to recover your funds if the device is lost or damaged.

  5. Add Cryptocurrency Accounts
    Once setup is complete, use Ledger Live to add accounts for various cryptocurrencies. Ledger supports a wide range of digital assets, from Bitcoin and Ethereum to numerous altcoins.

  6. Start Transacting Securely
    With your Ledger wallet set up, you can send, receive, and manage cryptocurrencies confidently. The hardware wallet ensures your private keys never leave the device, offering unmatched protection against online threats.
